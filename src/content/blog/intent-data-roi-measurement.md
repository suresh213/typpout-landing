## The Financial Metrics of Outbound Sales

To calculate outbound ROI, you must track three primary financial variables:

### 1. Cost Per Lead (CPL)
The total budget spent to acquire and enrich one target contact profile.
$$\text{CPL} = \frac{\text{Software Subscriptions + Database Access Fees}}{\text{Total Enriched Leads}}$$

### 2. Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) - Outbound Niche
The total sales and marketing budget spent to secure a single closed-won customer.
$$\text{Outbound CAC} = \frac{\text{Sales Tool Cost + Pro-rated Rep Salaries}}{\text{Total Closed-Won Customers}}$$

### 3. Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) to CAC Ratio
The indicator of long-term business health and profitability. High-performing B2B SaaS companies maintain an LTV:CAC ratio greater than **3:1**.

The massive difference in unit economics is driven by two key conversion levers:

With traditional outbound, you pay a junior SDR $5,000 a month to manually build, scrub, clean, and follow up on lists. 
* By deploying an autonomous AI SDR, you eliminate this administrative labor cost entirely, reducing your top-of-funnel CAC to near zero.
