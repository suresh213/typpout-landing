LinkedIn Sales Navigator and Typpout are both used for B2B prospecting, but they solve fundamentally different problems. Understanding the distinction helps you choose the right tool, or decide to use both together.

## What Sales Navigator does

Sales Navigator is LinkedIn's premium prospecting tool. It provides:

* **Advanced search filters**: Find prospects by job title, company size, industry, geography, seniority, and more.
* **Lead recommendations**: AI-suggested prospects based on your targeting criteria.
* **InMail credits**: Send messages to prospects outside your network.
* **Saved leads and alerts**: Track specific prospects and get notified of their activity.
* **TeamLink**: See which colleagues are connected to your target prospects.

Sales Navigator is excellent at answering: "Who could be my buyer?"

## What Typpout does

[Typpout](/) is a social intent selling platform. It provides:

* **Real-time social listening**: Monitor LinkedIn, X, and Instagram for buying intent signals.
* **ICP matching**: Automatically check each signal against your ideal customer profile.
* **Personalized outreach**: AI-generated messages grounded in the buyer's actual post content.
* **AI Reply Agent**: Autonomous conversation management from first reply to booked meeting.
* **Multi-platform coverage**: LinkedIn, X, and Instagram in a single platform.

Typpout answers: "Who is actively buying right now?"

## Using both together

Sales Navigator and Typpout work well as complementary tools:

* **Sales Navigator** for building target account lists, identifying key stakeholders, and researching prospects before meetings.
* **Typpout** for monitoring those accounts (and new ones) for real-time buying signals and automating engagement.

The combination gives you both the strategic targeting of Sales Navigator and the timing precision of Typpout's intent signals.
