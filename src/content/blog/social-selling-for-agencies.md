B2B marketing and sales agencies are always looking for services that deliver measurable pipeline results. Content marketing, paid ads, and SEO are valuable but take months to produce attributable revenue. Clients want faster results and clearer attribution.

Social selling as a service fills this gap. By monitoring social platforms for buying intent signals on behalf of clients and converting those signals into qualified meetings, agencies can offer a pipeline generation service with clear, weekly-measurable results.

## Why social selling works as an agency service

1. **Fast time to value.** Clients see qualified meetings within the first week, not months. This reduces churn and increases client satisfaction.
2. **Clear attribution.** Every meeting can be traced back to a specific social signal. No attribution ambiguity.
3. **Scalable operations.** A single operator can manage social selling for multiple clients using [Typpout](/), configuring different ICP criteria and signal keywords for each.
4. **Recurring revenue.** Signal monitoring and outreach is an ongoing service, not a one-time project.

## Setting up social selling for clients

### 1. ICP workshop
Run a structured session with each client to define their ideal customer profile including firmographic criteria and behavioral intent signals.

### 2. Signal library development
Build a library of keyword phrases and trigger events specific to the client's category and competitive landscape.

### 3. Configure Typpout
Set up [Typpout](/) with the client's ICP criteria and signal keywords. The platform handles 24/7 monitoring across LinkedIn, X, and Instagram.

### 4. Weekly reporting
Provide clients with weekly reports showing signals detected, outreach sent, replies received, and meetings booked. The metrics are straightforward and tied directly to pipeline.
