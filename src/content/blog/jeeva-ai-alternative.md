Jeeva AI has built a strong name in the AI SDR space — particularly for teams that want a fully autonomous outbound agent. But the AI SDR category exploded in 2025-2026, and "Jeeva alternative" is now one of the most-searched queries for GTM tooling.

This guide compares the strongest alternatives in 2026, with verified pricing and an honest take on which one fits which motion.

## Why GTM teams are evaluating Jeeva alternatives

Jeeva does one thing well — autonomous email outbound at scale. The questions teams ask when evaluating alternatives cluster into four themes:

- **Email-only ceiling.** Jeeva's core motion is email. Teams that have moved pipeline to LinkedIn and X want a single agent that operates across channels, not bolted-on integrations.
- **Signal blindness.** Jeeva sequences are list-driven. Teams want intent-driven, not list-driven, outbound.
- **Reply handling.** When prospects respond, Jeeva hands back to humans. In 2026, the best teams want the agent to handle first-reply qualification too.
- **PMM / GTM use cases.** Jeeva is sales-team-shaped. PMMs and founder-led GTM teams want a workspace, not a sequencer.

## 1. Typpout — for signal-based, multi-channel GTM

[Typpout](/) is the closest direct alternative to Jeeva for teams that want an AI-first GTM platform — but built around social signals instead of email sequences.

**Key features**

- **Social Listening Agent** across LinkedIn, X and Instagram (Jeeva is email-only).
- **ICP Finder Agent** that scores every signal against your buyer profile in real time.
- **Personalised Outreach Agent** that drafts messages using the prospect's own post / role / context.
- **AI Reply Agent** that responds within seconds — closes the loop Jeeva hands back to a human.

**Pricing.** $99 / mo (Solo) → $249 / mo (Growth) → $990 / mo (Done For You with a 15-meeting guarantee). [See full pricing](/pricing).

**Best for.** Founder-led GTM, PMMs, and B2B teams under $50M ARR. If even 40% of your buyers are on LinkedIn, Typpout typically beats Jeeva on cost-per-meeting in the first 30 days.

**Limitation.** If your motion is purely outbound email and your buyers are not on social, Jeeva's email orchestration is still strong.

## 2. Apollo — for high-volume cold email

Apollo combines a 250M+ contact database with a basic sequencer. It's the obvious starting point for high-volume, low-ACV teams.

**Best for.** SMB outbound teams running 5000+ sends per week. Apollo's data freshness has improved through 2025 and the price point ($59/mo) is hard to beat at the entry tier.

**Limitation.** Templated outbound at volume. Reply rates in 2026 are typically 0.5-1.5% — fine for high-volume motions, brutal for high-ACV ones.

## 3. Clay — for ops-led custom workflows

Clay is less a competitor and more a fundamentally different shape. It's a programmable data layer that ops teams use to build custom enrichment-and-outreach pipelines.

**Best for.** RevOps teams with engineering capacity who want full control. If you have a Clay power-user on the team, you can build anything.

**Limitation.** The learning curve is steep. Most GTM teams don't have an in-house Clay operator and the time-to-first-meeting is measured in weeks, not days.

## 4. HeyReach — for LinkedIn-only outbound

HeyReach is the leading LinkedIn automation tool for agencies and teams running multi-account LinkedIn outbound.

**Best for.** Pure LinkedIn outbound at scale, especially agencies with 10+ LinkedIn accounts to manage.

**Limitation.** LinkedIn-only. No signal layer — you're still working from lists. We've written more on the [HeyReach trade-off](/blogs/heyreach-alternative).

## 5. Lemlist — for friendly email + image personalisation

Lemlist popularised the personalised-cold-email aesthetic and remains strong for teams that want creative outbound (custom images, video).

**Best for.** Smaller teams who want their cold email to feel hand-crafted. Lemlist's personalisation features are still the most polished in the email space.

**Limitation.** Same email-only ceiling as Jeeva. No social signal layer.
