Dripify is a cloud-based LinkedIn automation tool designed for running drip campaigns. You build a sequence of LinkedIn actions like connection requests, messages, and profile views, set delays between steps, and Dripify executes them on autopilot.

The problem with drip campaigns on LinkedIn is the same as drip campaigns everywhere: the timing is arbitrary. A seven-day delay between follow-ups is based on your calendar, not the buyer's readiness. When messages arrive at random times relative to the buyer's actual interest, response rates suffer.

## Why teams move beyond Dripify

1. **Arbitrary timing.** Drip delays do not correlate with buyer intent. A prospect may be ready on day one or never, and the drip schedule ignores both cases.
2. **Account risk.** LinkedIn continues to tighten enforcement against automation. Cloud-based tools reduce some risk but do not eliminate it.
3. **No intent detection.** Dripify sends sequences to everyone on a list equally, regardless of who is actually in-market.
