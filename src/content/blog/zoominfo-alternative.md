ZoomInfo is the enterprise standard for B2B contact and company data. With over 100 million business profiles, technographic data, org charts, and a basic intent data layer, it provides the most comprehensive database available for enterprise sales teams.

The trade-off is cost. ZoomInfo contracts typically start at $15,000 per year and scale well above that for larger teams. For organizations with the budget, the data quality justifies the investment. For everyone else, the question is whether there are more efficient ways to find in-market buyers.

## Why teams look for ZoomInfo alternatives

1. **Cost.** Annual contracts starting at $15K+ make ZoomInfo inaccessible for startups, small teams, and early-stage companies.
2. **Static data limitations.** ZoomInfo tells you who someone is. It does not tell you whether they are actively evaluating solutions right now. Intent data is available but based on third-party aggregation, not real-time first-party signals.
3. **Data decay.** B2B contact data changes constantly. Job moves, email changes, and company restructures mean a portion of any database export is outdated by the time you reach out.
