Cognism is the leading B2B data provider for European and UK markets. Its phone-verified mobile numbers, GDPR compliance framework, and curated data quality make it the default choice for sales teams with phone-heavy outbound motions targeting EMEA.

But Cognism shares the same core limitation as every contact database: it tells you who someone is, not whether they are in-market right now. Verified phone numbers are valuable, but calling someone who is not thinking about your category produces the same result as emailing them, a low conversion rate and wasted effort.

## Why teams look for Cognism alternatives

1. **High cost.** Cognism is priced for enterprise budgets. Smaller teams struggle to justify the investment.
2. **Cold contacts.** Phone-verified does not mean interested. Most calls to database-sourced contacts interrupt someone who is focused on other priorities.
3. **Limited to data.** Cognism provides contacts. You still need separate tools for outreach, sequencing, and follow-up.
