The effectiveness of social selling depends on configuring the right keyword signals. Generic keywords like "looking for a tool" capture some intent, but industry-specific phrases capture far more.

Buyers in different industries use different language when they are evaluating solutions. Understanding these linguistic patterns is the difference between a social selling system that finds 10 signals per week and one that finds 100.

## SaaS / Software

**High-intent keywords:**
* "Evaluating [category] tools"
* "Switching from [specific tool]"
* "Alternative to [competitor]"
* "Our current [tool] is not scaling"
* "Comparing [Tool A] vs [Tool B]"
* "Building our tech stack for [year]"
* "Anyone use [tool] at [company size]?"
* "Our [tool] contract is up for renewal"

**Trigger events:**
* New CTO/CIO/VP Engineering hired
* Series A/B/C funding announced
* Company headcount doubled
* New product launch requiring infrastructure

## Fintech / Financial Services

**High-intent keywords:**
* "Compliance automation for [regulation]"
* "Modernizing our [banking/payment] infrastructure"
* "KYC/AML tool recommendations"
* "Core banking platform evaluation"
* "Embedded finance partner search"
* "Open banking API providers"

**Trigger events:**
* New regulatory requirement announced
* Banking charter application
* Partnership with financial institution
* Expansion to new market

## Cybersecurity

**High-intent keywords:**
* "SIEM replacement"
* "Zero trust implementation"
* "SOC automation tools"
* "Incident response platform"
* "Vulnerability management at scale"
* "Cloud security posture management"
* "Endpoint detection recommendations"

**Trigger events:**
* Data breach at competitor
* New vulnerability disclosure
* Compliance audit coming up
* New CISO hired

## HR Technology

**High-intent keywords:**
* "HRIS for [company size] employees"
* "ATS that integrates with [tool]"
* "Onboarding automation"
* "Performance review platform"
* "Employee engagement survey tool"
* "Payroll provider for [country/state]"
* "People analytics dashboard"

**Trigger events:**
* First VP of People hired
* Company passed 50/100/500 employees
* Remote-first policy announced
* Multiple open headcount posted
