Clay has become the go-to tool for RevOps and growth engineering teams that need to build custom lead enrichment workflows. Its ability to pull data from 50+ providers through a single interface and create programmable data waterfalls is genuinely powerful.

But Clay solves one specific problem: data enrichment. It does not source leads based on intent signals. It does not send outreach messages. It does not handle reply conversations or book meetings. You still need separate tools for every other step in the outbound process.

## Why teams look for Clay alternatives

1. **Enrichment without execution.** Clay enriches data beautifully but stops there. You need additional tools to actually reach out to the contacts you have enriched.
2. **Complexity.** Building Clay workflows requires technical skills. It is a builder's tool, not a plug-and-play solution.
3. **No intent sourcing.** Clay tells you more about contacts you already have. It does not find new contacts based on real-time buying behavior.
