Lusha is a popular contact enrichment tool that provides email addresses and phone numbers for B2B prospects. Its browser extension makes it easy to grab contact details from LinkedIn profiles, and its API integrates with most CRM and outreach tools.

For teams that need quick access to verified contact details, Lusha is affordable and straightforward. But contact details alone are not enough to generate pipeline efficiently. Knowing someone's email address tells you nothing about whether they are in a buying cycle right now.

## Why teams look beyond Lusha

1. **Data without intent.** Lusha gives you how to reach someone. It does not tell you whether you should reach out to them right now.
2. **Cold outreach still required.** After enriching contacts with Lusha, you still need to send cold messages to people who may not be in-market.
3. **Limited to contact data.** No social listening, no intent signals, no outreach automation.
